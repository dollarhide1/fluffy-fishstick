From Carol
"I supported my husband's career, raised four children — and still felt something missing."

At 45, I was a middle-aged woman in a 9-to-5 HR job, asking the question so many of us ask in private: is this all there is? For as long as I could remember, I wanted to be a nurse — but military moves packed the dream into boxes, four children meant the money was never quite there, and then age showed up as one more obstacle nobody warned me about.

The self-doubt almost won. Almost. I became a registered nurse at 50, and years later I'm still doing what I love — and still meeting women in their 30s, 40s, 50s, and 60s sitting exactly where I once sat. I built this system for them. For you.

You are not too late. You are right on time.
